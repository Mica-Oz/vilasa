NEILEY & MORRIS

RESOLUTION-DRIVEN LEGAL REPRESENTATION

Neiley & Morris is a boutique law firm representing clients in matters of Family Law and Criminal Defense. We provide a team of attorneys and staff who has extensive trial, evidentiary, and negotiation experience. Clients of Neiley & Morris receive reasoned legal advice and zealous advocacy as we help them navigate complex and difficult legal matters.

A team you can trust.

Advocacy

We work with our clients directly to understand their needs and goals. We advocate for justice and resolution that is client-focused.

Preparedness

We prepare our cases and clients by conducting early document review, investigation, and hands-on case management. From the outset, we familiarize ourselves with the legal issues and challenges that our clients face, and we are prepared to tackle them—big or small.

Integrity

We consider the short-term and long-term implications of our work and provide straightforward, honest legal advice to our clients. We guide and assist our clients in making important legal decisions.
